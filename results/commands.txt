Some settings for ants optimization
- Basic settings: Use insert heuristic, update with optimized solutions, 50 steps with no update until termination
sastpsolver -a 8 -I -H 1 -m 50 

- Use VND with Edge+Tour optimizer, update with global best, use only one best ant, upate pheromones with optimized solutions
src/sastpsolver -a 8 -vp -I -H 1 -m 50 -T 800 -D 600 -P 0.5 -N 10 -W 1 -U 2 -n 1 -z 3 sastp100.prob sastp100.sol

- Rank-AS setting (no max, W > 1, no global update)
src/sastpsolver -a 8 -vp -I -H 1 -z 3 -m 50 -P 0.8 -N 10 -W 5 sastp100.prob sastp100.sol

- MinMax-AS Setting (W = 1, no global update, use dynamic maxTau; dynamic minTau not implemented)
src/sastpsolver -a 8 -vp -I -H 1 -z 3 -m 50 -P 0.8 -N 10 -W 1 -C 10 -D -1 sastp100.prob sastp100.sol
